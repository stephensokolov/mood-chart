Baseline — Support

Baseline is a private mood & sleep tracker for iPhone.

Need help, found a bug, or have a suggestion? Email hello@sokolovlabs.com and I'll get back to you.

Quick help

  • Logging a day: tap any day to open its entry. Pick one mood tier for a single value, or two for a low-to-high range. Add sleep hours, symptom flags, and a note, then tap SAVE.
  • Week & month views: hold your phone in portrait for the week view; rotate to landscape for the month view.
  • Backing up: open SAVE/RESTORE to write a backup file, or EXPORT to share your history as CSV, JSON, or an image. Files are saved to your own device.
  • Restoring: use SAVE/RESTORE and choose Restore to load a backup file.

Your privacy

Baseline stores everything on your device and sends nothing to the developer. See the Privacy Policy for details.
