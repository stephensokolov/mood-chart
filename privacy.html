Privacy Policy

Synchronic Mood Tracker (the "App") collects no data.

All entries you make in the App — moods, sleep hours, symptom flags, and notes — are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. The App makes no network requests. Nothing is sent to the developer or to any third party.

You can export your data manually as a JSON or CSV file via the App's Save/Restore screen. These exports remain on your device unless you choose to share them through the iOS share sheet (e.g., to email yourself a backup).

The App does not use any third-party analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs.
